inputs

Player credentials
Player preference(game type, team, skill level)
Game commands(movement, action, chat messages)

Matchmaking algorithm (based on skill ratings like ELO or MMR)

Real-time communication (WebSocket handling for game & chat)
Anti-cheat verification (server-side validation of inputs)
Team coordination (chat and voice via channels)

Output:
Matched game session
Live game updates to all players
Player stats and leaderboards
Player interactions (chat messages, team info)
Warnings or bans for cheating
